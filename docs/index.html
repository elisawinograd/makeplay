#makeplay provides activities for busy parents with projects that encourage creativity, self-directed play and learning.
These are games made from recycled or found materials that require little craft or expense. Browse and enjoy!

The proliferation of touch tablets and cell phones has produced a generation of infants passively entertained through technology.

It is recommended that children under 2 have no screen time while older children receive less than two hours a day.

For more information on the topic of screen technology and child cognitive development click here.
