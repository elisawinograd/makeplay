Cereal Sculpture

Age: 6-8
Location: All
Set-up: Planned

Materials:

  • Scissors
  • Cereal box or any other thin cardboard box with available space to cut many small pieces

Set-up:

Cut out even squares and circles from the pieces of cardboard. Cut four 1 inch slits equal distances apart on each piece of cardboard.

Play:

Show your child how to start making a sculpture with the different pieces by locking one piece into the other with the slits. A good way of starting to build is to start with a central piece (a circular one usually works well) and more pieces to this central piece.
