Musical Can

Age: 6-8
Location: Indoor, Outdoor, Travel
Set-up: Planned

Materials:

  • Can
  • Thin stick or utensil to play the can with

Set-up:

Find an empty can and remove the lid completely. Make sure that the inside edges of the can are not sharp and hazardous for young children; if the can does have sharp edges, you can cover that end of the can with a piece of paper or aluminum foil and place a rubber band around it. This can be used a secondary "drum".

Play:

Using the stick or a utensil of your choice have you child explore the different sounds that can be make with the can. Your child can move the utensil from side to side on the side of the can or tap the top of can.
