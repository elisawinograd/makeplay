Catch the Ball

Age: 6-8
Location: Indoor, Outdoor
Set-up: Planned

Materials:

  • Cup
  • String
  • Aluminum foil
  • Scissors
  • Pointy utensil to make a hole at the bottom of the cup

Set-up:

Make a hole at the bottom of a cup. Cut a piece of string about 10 inches long.

Play:

Have your child stack the paper rolls by placing one end of paper roll with another using the slits.
