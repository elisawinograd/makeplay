Tracings

Age: 3-5
Location: Waiting, Travel, Indoor
Set-up: Improvised

Materials:

  • Pencil or Pen
  • Cup
  • Napkin
  • Water Bottle
  • Water Bottle Cap
  • Sugar Packet
  • Different sized coins
  • Anything else that can be traced

Set-up:

On a piece of paper trace each object with a pencil or pen.

Play:

Have your child match the object to the tracing. If he or she finished quickly, have him or she arrange the objects from tallest to shortest, or biggest to smallest.
