Pasta Necklace

Age: 3-5, 6-8
Location: Indoor
Set-up: Planned

Materials:

  • Any type of pasta or cereal that you can pass a string through
  • String or dental floss

Set-up:

Cut a piece of string large enough to make a necklace
Gather the pasta to make the necklace

Play:

Have your child put the objects through the slots. When he or she is done, dump them out and play again.
