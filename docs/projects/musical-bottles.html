Musical Bottles

Age: 3-5
Location: Indoor, Waiting
Set-up: Improvised

Materials:

  • Two water bottles or more
  • Pencil, stick or something to tap the water bottles with

Set-up:

Fill two water bottles or more with different levels of water. If you have access to more water bottles, look for different sizes and shapes. If you are at a restaurant or coffee shop, feel free to ask your waiter for a pen and explore some improvised sounds.

Play:

With a pencil or stick, have you child explore the different sounds each water bottle makes. Enjoy a day of musical fun by tapping the water bottles in different areas!
