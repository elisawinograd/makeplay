Cup Guitar

Age: 6-8
Location: All
Set-up: Planned

Materials:

  • Large plastic cup
  • 5 Rubber bands

Set-up:

Take a plastic cup, the bigger the better, and 5 rubber bands. Place the rubber bands around the cup one by one until you have created a sequence of rubber bands.

Play:

Have your child enjoy playing each rubber band and listening to the different sounds that it reveals.
