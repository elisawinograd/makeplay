Slot Machine

Age: 1-2
Location: Indoor
Set-up: Planned

Materials:

  • Container with plastic lid or cardboard box
  • Anything that can fit through the slot (cue-tips, cotton balls, bottle caps)

Set-up:

Open different sized holes and slits at the top of any container just big enough to slip in the objects you have.

Play:

Have your child put the objects through the slots. When he or she is done, dump them out and play again.
