Slider Cup

Age: 6-8
Location: All
Set-up: Planned

Materials:

  • Two small clear plastic cups or more
  • Permanent marker

Set-up:

On one cup, draw a trail. You can draw the trail as you wish, with steps going up or numbers to follow. On the second cup draw an object or character to follow the trail. This can be a car, a spider, a butterfly, or whatever your child wishes to draw. You can follow the drawings in these images or make up your own.

Play:

Place the cup with the object inside the cup with the trail. Have you child hold one cup with one hand and with the other, move the cup inside and follow the trail until he or she reaches the end. Enjoy an exciting game of concentration and fun!
