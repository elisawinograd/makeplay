Catch the Cup

Age: 6-8
Location: indoor
Set-up: improvised

Materials:

  • 7 toilet paper rolls or more
  • Scissors
  • Optional: Egg carton

Set-up:

Cut 4 evenly spaced slits at the top and bottom of each paper roll.
You can cut the part of an egg carton in which the egg is stored and use this to decorate other parts of the castle.

Play:

Have your child stack the paper rolls by placing one end of paper roll with another using the slits.
